How the search works

01 · Understand

Reads your request

Your words become a meaning fingerprint, so “shade-friendly greens” finds the right lettuces even without the word “lettuce.”

02 · Recall

Scans every variety

It compares that meaning against every product description in the catalog and pulls the closest candidates in milliseconds.

03 · Rank

Re-reads the top matches

Smart ranking re-reads your full request against each finalist and orders them by true relevance. Toggle it off to feel the difference.
